MY PERSONAL SOCCER TOURNAMENTS

My website provides information about my own international soccer results. This website is not about real World Cup results or any other tournaments. I just thought it will be fun to create my own tournaments.

The World Cup is the biggest sport's tournament in the world where the best 32 national soccer teams fight for the cup. The World Cup is held every four-years since its inaugural tournament in 1930. The most successful team is Brazil with 5 titles, and Italy and Germany are close behind with 4 titles each. In my league, the world cup takes place every four years starting in 1930 just like the original world cup. The best team in my league is Mexico with 3 tiles while the rest of the winners-Argentina, Uruguay, Spain, and Honduras- have one title each.

The Euro Cup was founded in 1928, and it is the biggest European soccer tournament. It is held every four years and it currently holds 24 teams in the Finals.

The Copa America is the oldest internacional tournament that has ever existed in this world. It is nearly 100 years old and it currently holds a 12-team format with 10 being south american countries while the other two are invited-teams usually from the North American confederation(CONCACAF). The most successful team is Uruguay with 15 tiles, and Argentina is the second successful team with 14. In my league, the Copa America started in 1927 and it is held every four years.
