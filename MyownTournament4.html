1928 Argentina Cup

The first ever Copa America took place in Argentina, one of the best teams in South America. They were favorites at the tournament, but the team ended with three loses in the gropu stage. The tournament held 12 teams including invited teams USA and Mexico. At the end, Brazil and Uruguay faced in the final in which Brazil won in penalty shootouts and that is when the rivalary between these teams first began.

1932 Bolivia Cup

The second edition took place in Bolivia and Brazil was expected to win. However, Brazil didn't passed the grop stage and Bolivia verly did. The host was the worst team to qualify to the quarter finals. However they made an impresive combat in the knockout stages, and Bolivia reached the final. They faced Ecuador who were the 2nd best team in the tournament. Bolivia fought to keep the cup, and they eventually did beating Ecuador 2-1. Bolivia became the first host country to win the cup in home soil out of any major tournament.
