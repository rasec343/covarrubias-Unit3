1928 Hungary Euro Cup

This was my first Euro cup, and I chose the host, Hungary, at random. In this tournament, 12 teams participated including Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Portugal, Russia, England, Croatia, Austria, Switzerland, and Greece. The tournament had a round-robin and knock-out format in which the teams were split into 3 groups of four. The top 8 teams with the highest point advanced to the knock-out stages to determine the tournaments champion. It was a tough battle for everyone, but at the end France and Spain fought for the cup in which France came victorios and won the first Euro Cup.

1932 Russia Euro Cup

The second edition of the Euro cup took place in Russian land in which the host country had high expectations to win the cup. The same 12 teams from the previous tornament participated for this cup. The host country was doing well until they lost in the quarter-finals against a Croatian team who verly made it to the knock-out stage. At the end, Germans faced Croatians in the final, and the German team won their first Euro cup in history.
